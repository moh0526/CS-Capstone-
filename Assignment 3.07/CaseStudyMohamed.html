Summary

Candy Crush is a free to play Match-3 puzzle game that provides thousands of puzzle levels for the user to complete. Candy Crush is easily accessible on Andriod and iOS platforms.
King is a video game developer company that developed Candy Crush.

Most Effective Methods


The thousands of unique levels provide users with a sense of accomplishment

As students become stressed, they can load up Candy Crush and recieve a sense of accomplishment as they complete each level; thus, destressing them.
Ultimately, the most effective thing Candy Crush provides to its user is the ability to destress.
Candy Crush's level design and game mechanics promote escapism for the user.

When a student is feeling stressed, they can use the Candy Crush app as a way of escapism because it will give them a sense of control.

Use of Technology


Users can play with their family and friends

Features that allow people to play with each other in Candy Crush promote community and can help decrease student's stress.
Teachnology can provide users with a sense of accomplishment and escapism, which allows them to destress.

Implications

Having fun, unique game mechanics can allow the user to relax and destress

My project will feature a game promoting relaxation and decreasing stress to help the users.
